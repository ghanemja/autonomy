Tello Edu Specifications

Features

5MP Camera - Supports Picture,Video, and Live Stream FPV (first person view)

Automatic Takeoff and Landing

Range Finder

Barometric Altimeter

LED Indicator

Bounce Mode

8 Different Flips, 360 Spin, Circle Fly

Speed Adjust

Up-Down, Forward-Backward, Left-Right, Yaw

Gyroscope Control

Supports Andriod, iOS, Tablets, and Smartphones

Bluetooth and Wi-Fi enabled

Code via Scratch, Swift, and Python

Charge via micro USB

Aircraft

Weight: 87 grams (propellers and battery included)

Dimensions: 98 x 92.5 x 41 mm

Propeller: 3 in [76.2 mm]

Battery: Detachable, 1.1Ah/3.8V, charge via Micro USB port, 1.5 hour charge time

Compatibility: Tello App and Tello Edu App (iOS and Andriod)

Flight Performance

Max Flight Distance: 100 meters

Max Speed: 8 m/s

Max Flight Time: 13 minutes

Max Flight Height: 30 meters

Camera

Photo: 5MP (2592x1936)

FOV (field of view): 82.6°

Video: HD 720P 30 fps

Format: JPG (Photo); MP4 (Video)

EIS (electronic image stabilization): Yes
